Lab Supplies

You are not required to purchase the lab items from the store/supplier suggested here. You may purchase them from anywhere of your choice as long as they meet the lab requirements.

Safety Items for SEP600 Labs

  1. Safety Glasses

    The safety glasses must have side shields and a top shield. (CCOHS Reference)

    Where to Buy: A similar version can be purchased from the Seneca Campus Store: Temp Lite Series Clear Safety Glasses.

SEP600 Microcontroller Board

You should already have the following microcontroller board from the SEH500 Microprocessor course. If you do not have one, you may purchase it from the vendor listed below or from your preferred electronics supplier.

FRDM-K64F or FRDM-K66F Freedom Development Platform.

Recommended Vendors* FRDM-K64F FRDM-K66F
Mouser FRDM-K64F FRDM-K66F
DigiKey FRDM-K64F FRDM-K66F
Newark FRDM-K64F FRDM-K66F

*If you purchase from the Canadian site of the recommended vendor above, you will need to pay HST, but they will not charge you import duties and fees. If you purchase from other online vendors, beware that import fees may apply.
